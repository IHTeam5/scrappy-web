Our Team

Meelod Waheed

Product Manager


Meelod Waheed is a first-generation Afghan-American at Northern Virginia Community College (NVCC). He is currently studying computer science, concentrating on Machine Learning research and optimization. After NVCC, he hopes to transfer into a top-twenty four-year university to continue pursuing Machine Learning and eventually get into computer vision and creative artificial intelligence. At NVCC, he currently serves as the President of the Phi Theta Kappa chapter, President of the STEAM Club, President of the Cloud Computing Club, and President of the Personal Finance Club. He is also a part of numerous other clubs and organizations at NVCC. He is the Head of Web Development and Design at The Caring Consultants. His role involves designing and maintaining the website with AWS, increasing their online presence, and editing/producing podcast episodes. After graduating, he hopes to become a Machine Learning engineer and start his own artificial intelligence firm.


Olufunke Ogunmefun

Designer


Olufunke Ogunmefun is a fourth year Computer Science major and Psychology minor at the University of Calgary in Calgary, Alberta and an international student from Lagos, Nigeria. Olufunke is passionate about the field of User Experience Design, having come across it during quarantine and discovering that it was the career she had been looking for, and has slowly built a digital portfolio after taking the opportunity to engage in self-directed learning about the field and taking part in design hackathons, as well completing relevant course projects. She volunteers in a number of student organizations at UCalgary, including as the Vice President, Communications for the Nigerian Students’ Association and a volunteer writer for The Gauntlet, the university’s student-led newspaper. She enjoys learning new languages, and tutors beginner-level French part-time as a way to help other students improve their French language skills and to continually improve her own. After graduation, Olufunke aims to begin her UX Design career in a client-focused and inclusive environment.

Stephanie Organista

Software Engineer


As a first-generation indigenous Latina college student, Stephanie hopes to offer unique perspectives in the field of tech. She is currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in computer science at the University of Houston. She has assumed leadership roles as Vice-President of Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society, through the Student Government Association as a Parliamentarian, and currently serves as SHPE Jr Chair for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE). She is also an active member of the Society of Women Engineers. She loves to be involved in projects that give back to her community. She has previously worked with a charity as a student mentor, where she tutored at-risk kids in low-income communities. Additionally, she is currently working on developing a youth STEM project to encourage Black/Latino high school students to pursue STEM careers.
